Creates a rectangle that can be used as a rendering destination to draw the video while maintaining aspect ratio. This will produce a rectangle that will fit inside the window area, but will maintain the aspect ratio of the video.

Parameters
video_widthThe width of the video.
video_heightThe height of the video.
window_widthThe width of the window, or the portion to use.
window_heightThe height of the window, or the portion to use.
window_xThe X offset of the window region to use.
window_yThe Y offset of the window region to use.
